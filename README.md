Graphical dashboard designed to visualize Facebook (Meta) stock performance, showcasing daily share prices alongside a 14-day moving average to highlight short-term trends. The project also incorporates daily total trading volume, offering insights into market activity. Data was sourced from Nasdaq's historical stock activity records.
Responsibilities:
• Collected and processed historical Facebook (Meta) stock data from NASDAQ for analysis.  
• Cleaned and transformed raw financial data, addressing issues such as currency symbols, numeric parsing, and missing values.  
• Developed a data preprocessing pipeline using Pandas to enable efficient time-series analysis.  
• Calculated a 14-day moving average to identify and evaluate short-term stock price trends.  
• Created time-series visualizations combining share price, moving average, and trading volume for comprehensive trend analysis.  
• Configured dual-axis plots to effectively display price trends alongside trading volume metrics.  
• Organized and sorted time-indexed data to ensure accuracy in chronological analysis.  
• Designed a dashboard-style plot with clear and readable visualizations to present key financial insights.
